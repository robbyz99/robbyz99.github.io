Frequently Asked Questions

What are your business hours?

We're open Monday through Friday from 8AM to 6PM, Saturday from 9AM to 4PM, and closed on Sundays. Emergency roadside assistance is available 24/7.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes, we work with all major insurance companies and can help you navigate the claims process for collision repairs and other covered services.

What types of vehicles do you service?

We service all types of vehicles, from economy cars to luxury and exotic vehicles. Our technicians are trained to work on all major brands and models.

How far in advance should I book a rental?

For luxury and exotic vehicles, we recommend booking at least 48-72 hours in advance. However, we often have vehicles available for same-day rentals.

Do you offer pickup and delivery services?

Yes, we offer pickup and delivery services for both rentals and service appointments within the NYC area. Additional fees may apply based on distance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and bank transfers. For rentals, a valid credit card is required for the security deposit.
